The Lidraughts forum is a public discussion board organized into categories. Players can post topics, reply to others, react to posts, and participate in team-specific forum sections.

Visit the forum at [lidraughts.org/forum](https://lidraughts.org/forum).

## Forum categories

The forum is organized into **categories** (`Categ`). Each category has a slug, name, description, and post/topic count. Categories are ordered by a `pos` field set by staff.

Special category types:

- **Staff forum** (`slug = "staff"`) — visible only to users with the `StaffForum` permission
- **Team forums** (`team` field set) — each team automatically gets a forum category with the slug `team-{teamId}`; accessible only to team members
- **Quiet categories** — posts in quiet categories do not appear in the recent posts feed or trigger timeline events (used for categories like `lidraughts-feedback` and `off-topic-discussion`)

## Posting and replying

### Creating a new topic

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a category">
    Navigate to the category where you want to post: `/forum/{categSlug}`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the topic form">
    Click **New topic** or go to `/forum/{categSlug}/form`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your post">
    Enter a topic title and body text. Topic names are normalized (no all-caps shouting).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    POST to `/forum/{categSlug}/new`. The topic and its first post are created together.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Replying to a topic

Open a topic at `/forum/{categSlug}/{slug}` and use the reply form at the bottom. POST to `/forum/{categSlug}/{slug}/new`.

<Note>
  To post in the forum you must have played at least one game **and** have had an account for at least 2 days. Titled players and verified accounts are exempt from this requirement.
</Note>

### Editing a post

You can edit your own post within **3 hours** of creation. The edit form is shown for up to 3 hours; the post can actually be saved for up to **4 hours**. Up to 5 previous versions are stored in the post's edit history. POST to `/forum/post/{id}` to submit an edit.

## Forum search

Search across all public forum posts at `/forum/search?text={query}&page={n}`. If the query is empty, the search page falls back to listing all studies — for the forum index, use `/forum` instead.

## Post reactions

Users can react to any forum post with the following reactions:

| Reaction | Type |
|---|---|
| `+1` | Positive |
| `-1` | Negative |
| `laugh` | Positive |
| `thinking` | Neutral |
| `heart` | Positive |
| `horsey` | Positive |

React via `POST /forum/post/{id}/react/{reaction}/{v}` where `v` is `true` to add or `false` to remove. When a post is edited, negative reactions (`-1`) are stripped from the stored reactions.

## Moderation

Forums can be moderated by Lidraughts staff (users with the `ModerateForum` permission) and by team owners in their team's forum category.

### Available moderation actions

<Accordion title="Close a topic">
  Toggle a topic's closed state via `POST /forum/{categSlug}/{slug}/close`. Closed topics prevent new replies. The action is logged by the mod log.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Hide a topic">
  Toggle a topic's visibility on the forum home via `POST /forum/{categSlug}/{slug}/hide`. Hidden topics are not shown in the recent posts feed. All posts in the topic are hidden from non-staff as well. Logged in the mod log.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Sticky a topic">
  Toggle sticky status via `POST /forum/{categSlug}/{slug}/sticky`. Sticky topics are shown at the top of the category page, above the regular paginated list. Logged in the mod log.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Delete a post">
  Delete any post via `POST /forum/{categSlug}/delete/{id}`. If the deleted post is the **first post** in a topic, the entire topic is deleted. Otherwise, only the post is removed and the topic/category counts are recalculated. Deletions are logged with the post text for audit purposes.
</Accordion>

<Warning>
  Moderation actions in team forums are available to **team owners** (not just global moderators). Global moderators can moderate any category.
</Warning>

## Team forums

Every Lidraughts team automatically has a forum category at `/forum/team-{teamId}`. Only team members can post in a team forum; the category is readable by everyone.

Messages posted to team forums are recorded separately from public forum messages for the `shutup` (abuse detection) system.

## Mentions

You can mention other users in a post with `@username`. The `MentionNotifier` sends a notification to each mentioned user when the post is created.

## FAQ

<Accordion title="Why can't I post in the forum?">
  You need to have played at least one game and have had your account for at least 2 days. Titled players and verified accounts bypass this restriction.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I post anonymously?">
  No. You must be logged in to create topics or reply. Anonymous users can read public forums but cannot post.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Why is my post not showing on the forum home?">
  Posts in categories like `lidraughts-feedback` and `off-topic-discussion` are automatically hidden from the home page if they are the first post in a new topic. Topics older than 5 days or topics with many replies may also be hidden. Moderators can un-hide topics manually.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I report a post?">
  Use the **Report** button on the post, or contact a moderator directly. Global moderators can see and act on reported content.
</Accordion>
