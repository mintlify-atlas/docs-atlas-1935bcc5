Teams let players group together around a shared interest, club, or federation. Teams have their own page, chat, member list, forum category, and can organize team tournaments and team battles.

Browse all teams at [lidraughts.org/team](https://lidraughts.org/team).

## Creating a team

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the team creation form">
    Navigate to `/team/new`. You must be logged in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    Provide a team name, optional location, and description. Choose whether membership is **open** (anyone can join immediately) or **closed** (requires approval).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Solve the CAPTCHA and submit. The team URL slug is derived from the name.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  You can only create **one team per week**. Coaches and staff with the `ManageTeam` permission are exempt from this limit.
</Warning>

## Joining a team

How you join a team depends on whether it is open or closed:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Open team">
    Click **Join** on the team page. You are added immediately. POST to `/team/{id}/join`.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Closed team">
    Click **Apply** to submit a join request. You are prompted to write a message explaining why you want to join (`/team/{id}/request/new`). The team leader reviews the request at `/team/requests` and can accept or decline.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

You can cancel a pending request or leave a team at any time via `/team/{id}/quit`.

<Info>
  You can subscribe to or unsubscribe from a team's PM notifications via `POST /team/{teamId}/subscribe`.
</Info>

## Team management

The team creator (owner) has access to administrative actions:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Edit team" icon="pen">
    Update the name, description, location, open/closed status, and chat settings at `/team/{id}/edit`
  </Card>
  <Card title="Kick a member" icon="user-minus">
    Remove a member from the team at `/team/{id}/kick`
  </Card>
  <Card title="Change owner" icon="arrow-right-arrow-left">
    Transfer ownership to another member at `/team/{id}/changeOwner`
  </Card>
  <Card title="PM all members" icon="envelope">
    Send a private message to all subscribed members at `/team/{id}/pm-all`
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Kicking members

The owner can remove any member from the team:

- Via the kick form at `GET /team/{id}/kick` (select from a list)
- Via the API at `POST /team/{id}/kick/{user}` (requires the `Team.Write` OAuth scope and team ownership)

Kicks by non-creator moderators are logged in the mod log.

### Changing ownership

To transfer team ownership, go to `/team/{id}/changeOwner` and select a current member. The previous owner remains a member. Ownership changes are logged.

## Team messaging (PM all)

Team owners can send a private message to all members who have **subscribed** to team messages:

- Navigate to `GET /team/{id}/pm-all`
- Compose the message and submit via `POST /team/{id}/pm-all`
- The message is automatically appended with a link back to the team page

<Warning>
  PM-all is rate-limited to **6 messages per 24 hours** per team leader.
</Warning>

## Team tournaments and team battles

Teams can host tournaments in two formats:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Team tournaments">
    Standard arena or Swiss tournaments restricted to team members. Create via `/tournament/new` and associate a team. The team's upcoming tournaments are shown on the team page and in the PM-all form.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Team battles">
    Multiple teams compete head-to-head, with each team's score tallied from its top players. Create via `/tournament/team-battle/new/{teamId}` or edit an existing battle at `/tournament/team-battle/edit/{id}`.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

All tournaments associated with a team are listed at `/team/{id}/tournaments`.

## WFD integration

Certain teams are designated as **WFD (World Draughts Federation)** teams. These teams have additional features:

- Members can have WFD player profiles attached to their Lidraughts account
- The owner (or a user with the `ManageWfd` permission) can edit WFD profiles at `/team/{id}/wfd/{userId}/profile`
- WFD team members are displayed with their official federation names where applicable
- WFD teams do not show team battle tournaments in the PM-all form

<Info>
  WFD status is set by Lidraughts staff. The `isWfd` flag on a team enables the WFD profile management routes.
</Info>

## Team chat

If the team's `chat` setting is enabled, members can chat on the team page in real time via WebSocket (`/team/{id}/socket/v{apiVersion}`). Kid-mode accounts cannot access team chats.

## Team API

The following public API endpoints are available for teams:

| Endpoint | Description |
|---|---|
| `GET /api/team/all` | Paginated list of all teams by popularity |
| `GET /api/team/search?text=` | Search teams by name |
| `GET /api/team/:id` | Team details including joined/requested status |
| `GET /api/team/:id/users` | Stream of team member user objects |
| `GET /api/team/of/:username` | All teams a user belongs to |
