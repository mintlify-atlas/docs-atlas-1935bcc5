This guide walks you through creating an account, choosing a variant, and starting your first game on Lidraughts.

<Note>
  You can play anonymously without an account, but creating one lets you track your rating, save games, join tournaments, and more.
</Note>

## Start Playing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Visit [lidraughts.org/signup](https://lidraughts.org/signup) and choose a username and password. No email address is required, though adding one lets you reset your password.

    Already have an account? [Sign in](https://lidraughts.org/login) instead.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up your preferences">
    Once signed in, open your [preferences](https://lidraughts.org/account/preferences) to configure:

    - **Display language** — choose from 27 available languages
    - **Board theme and piece set** — pick a visual style that suits you
    - **Clock and animation settings** — adjust how the board behaves during play
    - **Notification preferences** — control challenge and move alerts
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a game variant">
    Lidraughts supports seven variants across 10×10 and 8×8 boards:

    | Variant | Board | Description |
    |---|---|---|
    | Standard | 10×10 | International draughts |
    | Frisian | 10×10 | Extended capture directions |
    | Frysk! | 10×10 | 5 pieces per side, fast games |
    | Antidraughts | 10×10 | Force your opponent to take your pieces |
    | Breakthrough | 10×10 | First to promote a king wins |
    | Russian | 8×8 | Russian rules on a smaller board |
    | Brazilian | 8×8 | Brazilian rules on a smaller board |

    If you are new to draughts, start with **Standard**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start a game">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Play vs Human">
        Go to the [lobby](https://lidraughts.org/) to find a live opponent:

        1. Click **Create a game** and set your time control, variant, and preferred color.
        2. Your seek will appear in the lobby. When another player accepts, the game starts automatically.

        Alternatively, browse existing seeks in the lobby and click one to accept it directly.

        <Tip>
          Use the **Quick pairing** buttons at the top of the lobby to jump into a game instantly with common time controls.
        </Tip>
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Play vs Computer">
        Visit [lidraughts.org/setup/ai](https://lidraughts.org/setup/ai) to play against the Scan 3.1 engine:

        1. Select a **variant** (Standard, Frisian, Frysk!, Antidraughts, Breakthrough, or a custom position).
        2. Choose a **difficulty level** from 1 (easiest) to 8 (strongest).
        3. Set a **time control** — Unlimited, Real-time, or Correspondence.
        4. Pick your **color** and click **Play**.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Join a tournament">
    Tournaments run around the clock for all variants. Visit [lidraughts.org/tournament](https://lidraughts.org/tournament) to see upcoming and ongoing events:

    - **Arena tournaments** — join at any time; play as many games as possible in the allotted time.
    - **Swiss tournaments** — fixed number of rounds with paired opponents.

    Click any tournament to view the details, then click **Join** before the start time. You can also create your own team tournaments from the [teams](https://lidraughts.org/team) page.
  </Step>
</Steps>
