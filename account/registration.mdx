## Creating an account

Anyone can register a free Lidraughts account at [lidraughts.org/signup](https://lidraughts.org/signup). No payment or personal information beyond a username and email address is required.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Fill in the signup form">
    Navigate to `/signup` and provide:
    - **Username** — your public display name on the site
    - **Email address** — used for account recovery and optional notifications
    - **Password** — choose a strong, unique password
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pass the CAPTCHA">
    Complete the reCAPTCHA challenge to confirm you are not a bot. The signup form uses Google reCAPTCHA on the web.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your email">
    Depending on your IP address and browser fingerprint, Lidraughts may require email confirmation before allowing login. Check your inbox for a confirmation link sent to the address you provided.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start playing">
    After confirmation (or immediately if none was required), you are redirected to your profile page and can start playing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Mobile app signups always require email confirmation before the account becomes active.
</Note>

## Logging in

Visit `/login` and enter your **username or email address** together with your password. If you have two-factor authentication enabled, you will also be prompted for your TOTP code.

After a successful login you are redirected back to where you came from, or to the lobby if no referrer is present.

<Warning>
  Login attempts are rate-limited per IP address and username to protect against brute-force attacks. Too many failed attempts will result in a temporary block.
</Warning>

## Password reset

If you forget your password, use the self-service reset flow:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request a reset link">
    Go to `/password/reset` and enter the email address associated with your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email">
    Lidraughts sends a time-limited reset link to the address on file. The sent-confirmation page at `/password/reset/sent/:email` confirms dispatch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a new password">
    Click the link in the email to reach `/password/reset/confirm/:token`. Enter and confirm your new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Completing a password reset also disables two-factor authentication and signs out all existing sessions, so you start fresh.
</Info>

## Two-factor authentication (2FA)

Lidraughts supports TOTP-based two-factor authentication using any standard authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.).

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Enabling 2FA">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the 2FA settings page">
        Navigate to `/account/twofactor`. If 2FA is not yet enabled you will see the setup form.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Scan the QR code">
        Scan the displayed QR code with your authenticator app, or enter the secret key manually.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verify with a code">
        Enter the 6-digit code shown in your authenticator app together with your current password to confirm setup.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Done">
        2FA is now active. All existing sessions except the current one are signed out automatically.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Disabling 2FA">
    Go to `/account/twofactor` while logged in. You will be shown a disable form that requires your current password and a valid TOTP code. Submit the form to remove 2FA from your account.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
  Store your authenticator app backup codes in a safe place. If you lose access to your authenticator and cannot log in, a moderator can disable 2FA for you via the mod tools.
</Warning>

## Account security & session management

The security dashboard at `/account/security` lists all active login sessions, including the approximate location and device for each one.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Sign out one session" icon="right-from-bracket">
    Click the sign-out button next to any individual session to revoke it immediately. The session ID is sent to `POST /account/signout/:sessionId`.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sign out all other sessions" icon="shield-xmark">
    Use the **"Sign out all other devices"** option (`sessionId = "all"`) to revoke every session except your current one.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Kid mode

Kid mode restricts communication features to keep younger players safe. When enabled, chat and messaging are disabled.

- Toggle kid mode at `/account/kid`.
- The current state can be read and written via the API at `GET /api/account/kid` and `POST /api/account/kid?v=true` (requires the `preference:read` / `preference:write` OAuth scope).

<Tip>
  Parents and educators can enable kid mode on a shared account before handing a device to a child.
</Tip>

---

## Email address management

Change your registered email address at `/account/email`. Lidraughts sends a confirmation link to the new address; the change takes effect only after you click that link.

Your current email can also be retrieved through the API at `GET /api/account/email` (requires the `email:read` OAuth scope).

---

## Closing your account

You can close your account permanently from `/account/close`. You will be asked to confirm using the account's password. Closed accounts can be reopened by a moderator upon request.
