Lidraughts is free, open-source, and ad-free. It is kept alive entirely by voluntary donations from the community. Supporters are called **patrons**.

<Note>
  Becoming a patron is optional. Every feature on Lidraughts is available to all users, whether they donate or not.
</Note>

---

## What patrons receive

Patrons receive a small visual recognition for their support:

- A **patron badge** (a wing icon) displayed next to their username in profiles, leaderboards, and game pages
- The satisfaction of keeping Lidraughts running and free for everyone

<Info>
  There are no gameplay advantages, premium game modes, or paywalled features. Patronage is purely a way to say thank you.
</Info>

---

## How to become a patron

Visit [lidraughts.org/patron](https://lidraughts.org/patron) to make a contribution.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Monthly support (Stripe)">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Choose a monthly amount">
        Select one of the suggested amounts (€5, €10, €20, €50) or enter a custom value.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter your payment details">
        Provide your card details via the Stripe-powered checkout form. Your email address is optional but recommended for Stripe receipts.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm the subscription">
        Submit the form. Your account is upgraded to patron status immediately and renews automatically each month.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    Your monthly amount can be adjusted at any time from the patron page using the **switch plan** option (`POST /patron/switch`).
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="One-time support (Stripe)">
    Select the **one-time** frequency option on the patron page and provide your card details. Your patron badge remains active for approximately one month after the charge.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="PayPal">
    Lidraughts also accepts payments through PayPal. The PayPal option appears on the patron page alongside Stripe. Both one-time and recurring PayPal subscriptions are supported.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Info>
  Payments are processed in **euros (EUR)**. The minimum contribution is €1.00; the maximum per transaction is €100,000.
</Info>

---

## Lifetime patron status

A single contribution of **€250 or more** (i.e. 25,000 cents) grants lifetime patron status. Lifetime patrons keep their badge permanently, regardless of future payment activity.

---

## Canceling a subscription

To cancel a recurring monthly subscription:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the patron page">
    Navigate to `/patron` while logged in. If you have an active Stripe subscription, your current plan details are shown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel the subscription">
    Click **Cancel** (submits to `POST /patron/cancel`). Your patron badge remains active until the end of the current billing period.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Cancellation stops future charges but does not trigger a refund for the current period.
</Warning>

## Patron list

The patron page shows two public lists:

- **Recent supporters** — users who contributed recently
- **Top supporters** — users with the highest cumulative support

Your appearance on these lists is automatic and tied to your account.

## Frequently asked questions

<Accordion title="Is my payment information stored on Lidraughts servers?">
  No. Card details are handled entirely by Stripe or PayPal and never pass through Lidraughts servers. Lidraughts only stores a Stripe customer ID or PayPal subscription ID to identify your account.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I donate on behalf of someone else?">
  One-time contributions can be made without a Lidraughts account. The patron badge, however, is only awarded to an account that is logged in at the time of the donation.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if my card is declined?">
  Stripe will retry failed charges automatically. If a charge cannot be collected, your patron status will eventually expire. You can update your payment method directly in Stripe.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I donated but my badge is not showing.">
  Patron status is synced automatically each time you visit the patron page. If your badge is still missing after a few minutes, visit `/patron` to trigger a manual sync.
</Accordion>
