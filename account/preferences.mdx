All user preferences are accessible under `/account/preferences/:categ`. Changes are applied immediately and persisted to your account.

Preferences are organised into four categories:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Game display" icon="display" href="/account/preferences#game-display">
    Board theme, piece set, animation, coordinates, and visual options.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Draughts clock" icon="clock" href="/account/preferences#draughts-clock">
    Clock display, sound, tenths of seconds, and more-time settings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Game behavior" icon="gamepad" href="/account/preferences#game-behavior">
    Move input, premoves, takebacks, resignation confirmation, and more.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Privacy" icon="lock" href="/account/preferences#privacy">
    Challenge, messaging, study invitations, and insight-sharing controls.
  </Card>

## Game display

Route: `GET /account/preferences/game-display`

<Accordion title="Board theme">
  Choose the colour scheme of the board. Available themes: `blue`, `blue2`, `blue3`, `canvas`, `wood`, `wood2`, `wood3`, `maple` (default), `brown`, `brown2`, `leather`, `match`, `ic`, `green`, `marble`, `grey`, `metal`, `olive`, `purple`.

  You can also switch to a **transparent** or **dark** background, or provide a custom background image URL.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Piece set">
  Choose the visual style of the pieces. Available piece sets: `wide_crown` (default), `wide`, `narrow_edge`, `narrow`, `fabirovsky`, `flat`, `phin`, `ringed`, `basic`, `frisianovsky`, `eightbit`.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Coordinate system">
  Switch between **Fieldnumbers** (1–50 notation) and **Algebraic** notation for board coordinates.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Coordinates display">
  Show board coordinate labels: **No**, **Inside the board**, or **Outside the board** (default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Piece animation">
  Control the speed of piece movement animations: **None**, **Fast**, **Normal** (default), or **Slow**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Show captured pieces">
  Display a tray of captured pieces alongside the board (enabled by default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="King move counter">
  Show the king-move counter used in endgame rules (enabled by default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Move highlights">
  Highlight the last move on the board (enabled by default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Destination squares">
  Highlight legal destination squares when a piece is selected (enabled by default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Game result notation">
  Choose how results are displayed: standard **1-0 / ½-½ / 0-1** or draughts-style **2-0 / 1-1 / 0-2** (default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Move replay">
  Automatically replay moves after a game ends: **Never**, **On slow games**, or **Always** (default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Zen mode">
  Hide distracting UI elements during a game. Can also be toggled quickly via `POST /pref/zen`.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Blindfold mode">
  Hide the pieces during play for a training challenge.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Board resize handle">
  Show the drag handle for resizing the board: **Never**, **On initial position** (default), or **Always**.
</Accordion>

## Draughts clock

Route: `GET /account/preferences/draughts-clock`

<Accordion title="Clock tenths of seconds">
  Show tenths of a second on the clock: **Never**, **When time remaining < 10 seconds** (default), or **Always**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Clock bar">
  Display a graphical bar showing remaining time (enabled by default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Clock ticking sound">
  Play a ticking sound when the clock is running low (enabled by default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Give more time">
  Allow giving extra time to your opponent in correspondence games: **Never**, **In casual games only** (default in casual), or **Always**.
</Accordion>

## Game behavior

Route: `GET /account/preferences/game-behavior`

<Accordion title="Move input method">
  How you move pieces: **Click two squares**, **Drag a piece**, or **Both clicks and drag** (default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Premove">
  Allow queuing a move before your opponent has finished moving (enabled by default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Takeback">
  Allow your opponent to request a takeback (undo): **Never**, **In casual games only**, or **Always** (default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Auto-claim threefold repetition">
  Automatically claim a draw on threefold repetition: **Never**, **Always**, or **When time remaining < 30 seconds** (default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Submit move confirmation">
  Show a confirmation button before committing a move: **Never**, **Correspondence games only** (default), **Correspondence and unlimited**, or **Always**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Confirm resignation">
  Ask for confirmation before resigning (enabled by default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Keyboard move input">
  Enable moving pieces by typing square numbers on the keyboard (disabled by default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Full capture enforcement">
  Require the longest capture sequence in variants where this is mandatory (disabled by default).
</Accordion>

## Privacy

Route: `GET /account/preferences/privacy`

<Accordion title="Follow activity">
  Let followed players see when you are active (enabled by default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Who can challenge you">
  Control who may send you game challenges: **Never**, **If rating is ±300**, **Only friends**, or **Always** (default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Who can message you">
  Control who may send you private messages: **Never**, **Only friends**, or **Always** (default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Who can invite you to studies">
  Control who may invite you to collaborative studies: **Never**, **Only friends**, or **Always** (default).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Share your game insights">
  Allow others to view your game-analysis insights: **With nobody**, **With friends** (default), or **With everybody**.
</Accordion>

## Additional customization

### Sound set

Change the move and game-event sounds via `POST /pref/soundSet`. Available sound sets: **Silent**, **Standard** (default), **Piano**, **NES**, **SFX**, **Futuristic**, **Robot**, **Pentatonic**, **Speech**.

### Language

Select the interface language at `POST /translation/select`. Lidraughts will remember your choice across sessions.

### Profile

Edit your public profile at `/account/profile`:

- **Bio** — a short description shown on your profile page
- **Location** — city or country
- **Country flag** — shown next to your username in leaderboards
- **FIDE rating** — your over-the-board rating
- **Title** — official draughts or FMJD title (requires moderator verification)

### Username casing

Adjust the capitalisation of your username (not the username itself) at `/account/username`.

## API access to preferences

```bash
# Read your preferences
curl https://lidraughts.org/api/account/preferences \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer your_token_here"
```

Reading preferences requires the `preference:read` OAuth scope. Writing preferences requires `preference:write`.
