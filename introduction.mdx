Lidraughts is a free, open-source, and ad-free online draughts server available at [lidraughts.org](https://lidraughts.org). No accounts are required to play, no ads are ever shown, and the full source code is publicly available under the AGPL-3.0 license.

## What is Lidraughts?

Lidraughts is a rewrite of [lichess/lila](https://github.com/ornicar/lila/) adapted for draughts, supporting both 10×10 and 8×8 boards. It is built on the Play 2.x/Scala framework with a MongoDB backend and a TypeScript/SCSS frontend, and uses the [Scan 3.1](https://github.com/rhalbersma/scan) engine for computer opposition and analysis.

## Supported Variants

Lidraughts supports the following draughts variants:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Standard (10×10)" icon="chess-board">
    International draughts on the full 10×10 board — the most widely played form of the game.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Frisian" icon="flag">
    A 10×10 variant where kings and pieces can capture in additional directions, leading to explosive tactics.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Frysk!" icon="bolt">
    Starts with only 5 pieces per side on the back rank — a fast-paced 10×10 variant.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Antidraughts" icon="shuffle">
    The goal is reversed: force your opponent to capture all your pieces first.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Breakthrough" icon="arrow-up">
    First player to promote a piece to king wins.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Russian (8×8)" icon="chess-board">
    Russian draughts on an 8×8 board, with its own promotion and capture rules.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Brazilian (8×8)" icon="chess-board">
    Brazilian draughts on an 8×8 board, closely related to Russian draughts.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Key Features

- **Live games** — play real-time games against other players from the lobby
- **Computer opponents** — challenge the Scan 3.1 engine at 8 difficulty levels
- **Tournaments** — Arena and Swiss-format tournaments for all variants
- **Simultaneous exhibitions** — one player faces many opponents at once
- **Tactics puzzles** — solve positions for Standard, Frisian, and Russian variants
- **Board editor** — set up any position from scratch
- **Analysis board** — review games with engine assistance
- **Studies** — create and share annotated analysis with others
- **Coordinates training** — learn board coordinates
- **Forums and teams** — community discussion and group play
- **Open API** — programmatic access to games, users, and more
- **No ads, no tracking** — fully free and privacy-respecting
